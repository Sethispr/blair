We value your trust. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect
The bot may collect and store the following when you use it
- Your Discord user ID
- Your server IDs
- Commands you use
- Your card collection, inventory and trade history
- Profile card settings
- Any profile card images you provided to the bot.

**We do not collect anything else.**

## How Long We Keep Your Data
Active data (collection, inventory, profile) is kept as long as you use the bot.  
If you stop using the bot for more than 2 years, we automatically delete everything except basic stats.  
You can ask for full deletion at any time, we try to do it within 7 days.

## Discord Permissions
The bot only asks for permissions it needs to work (read messages in channels where it is used, send messages, manage embeds).  
It never asks for access to your private DMs or other servers.

## Minors / COPPA
If you are under 13 years old, you are not allowed to use the bot. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## How We Use Your Information
We use this information only to
- Provide and operate the bot's services
- Fix bugs and functions
- Improve the bot's speed and efficiency

## Data Sharing
**We do not sell or share your personal information with anyone.**
Your data will only be shared with third parties if we are required to do so by law.

## Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no online service is completely secure.

## Your Control
You can stop using the bot at any time.  
If you request user data deletion, we will try to remove all stored information about you within 7 days.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of the page will always show the last update.
